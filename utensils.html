Coffee Grinder

Fresh coffee is the best wake up remedy around, especially when you get to grind it fresh yourself every morning.

French Press

Serious about the taste of your coffee? French pressing it is the absolute best way to bring out all the cupping notes. It takes a little more effort for a vast difference in flavor.

Goose Neck Kettle

While it doesn't have to be a Goose Neck style kettle, being able to get your water hot for your french press quickly is important!

Coffee Scale

For the most astout coffee heads, weighing your beans out to make sure that your bean to water ratio is just right. I myself enjoy about 5 oz of beans to 3 cups of water.
